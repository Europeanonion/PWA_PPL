Phase 1 - Base Hypertrophy (Moderate Volume, Moderate Intensity)
Push #1
Exercise Warm-up Working Reps RPE Rest
Bench Press i 2 1 3-5 8-9 ~3-5 min
Notes: Set up a comfortable arch, quick pause on the chest and explode up on each rep

Substitution Options:
1. DB Bench Press
2. Machine Chest Press
High-Incline Smith Machine Press i 2 2 4-6 10 ~3-4 min
Notes: Set the bench at a 45-60° incline, touch the bar to your upper chest with control

Substitution Options:
1. Incline DB Press
2. Incline Machine Press
Egyptian Cable Lateral Raise i 2 2 6-8 10 ~2-3 min
Notes: Lean away from the cable. Focus on squeezing your delts.

Substitution Options:
1. DB Lateral Raise
2. Machine Lateral Raise
Overhead Cable Triceps Extension i 2 2 4-6 10 ~2-3 min
Notes: Do both arms at once, resist the negative

Substitution Options:
1. DB Floor Skull Crusher
2. DB French Press

Notes: Lean slightly forward, lock your elbow behind your torso (shoulder hyperextension)

Substitution Options:
1. DB Triceps Kickback
2. Triceps Pressdown
Pull #1

2. Machine Pulldown

1. Meadows Row
2. Single-Arm Row

Notes: Swing the weight "out", not "back"

Substitution Options:
1. Reverse Cable Flye
