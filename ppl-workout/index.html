RPE Scale Guide

RPE (Rate of Perceived Exertion) is a scale used to measure the intensity of your exercise.

RPEDescriptionReps in Reserve
10Maximum effort0 - Could not do more reps
9Very hard effort1 - Could do 1 more rep
8Hard effort2 - Could do 2 more reps
7Moderate effort3 - Could do 3 more reps
6Somewhat hard4 - Could do 4 more reps
5Light effort5 - Could do 5 more reps

This program primarily uses RPE 8-9 for main lifts and RPE 9 for accessory exercises.
